Resource Profile: FR Core Practitioner Role Profession

Official URL: https://hl7.fr/ig/fhir/core/StructureDefinition/fr-core-practitioner-role-profession Version: 2.0.0
Draft as of 2023-11-17 Computable Name: FrCorePractitionerRoleProfession

Profile of the PractitionerRole resource. This profile specifies the professions of the practitioner authorized for France Spécification du profil de la ressource PractitionerRole permettant de spécifier la ou les professions du PS autorisées pour un exercice en France.

Usage:
